Indian cooking for absolute beginners

On this small website I collect my experience with indian cooking for people who experiment with it like me outside of India. This site is not a substitute for professional sites on Indian cooking, but it shows him how to approach this topic if you are neither a cook nor have learned this kind of cooking already at home.

How I came to this topic

I came to the topic of indian cooking mainly because one of my sons started eating only vegetarian at an early age. I wanted to cook something for him that wasn't just a poorer substitute for dishes with meat. I experimented somewhat fruitlessly with Indian recipes but then learned mostly through a cookbook, to cook things thatwere quite well received at least by my son and by my guests. I still cook mainly with this cookbook but I have learned a lot more in the meantime.
